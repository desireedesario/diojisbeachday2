Birthday Beach Bash!

ABOUT BEACH BASH
Ladies and gentlemen, boys and girls, puppies of all ages!!! I, your host Dioji, welcome you to the most glorious beach day! Hear the rocking music, run around with your fellow friendly dogs, eat and snack on the most incredible of treats and food to your heart's desire, and marvel at what will surely be the most spectacular birthday beach celebration!!!
We are celebrating the birthdays of the dogs of October, including Keiko, Eevee, and of course, yours truly! We’ll be on the sand Rosie's dog beach near the main building where the restrooms and food stands are. We’ll have two large canopies for humans and dogs, along with food, water, and beach blankets.



ABOUT RAFFLE

We’ll be having a raffle at noon, and a potluck so we can eat! Bring items for these if you have a chance, but if you can’t, make sure you bring yourself and your dog anyway!
Raffle drawing at noon!



We’re trying to make the raffle as fair as possible by first giving out tickets to those who bring raffle items. People who bring potluck items will also get tickets. You can give away your tickets if you like. Extra tickets will be distributed randomly, or at least chaotically. You can bring used or new items. This is your chance to get that thing your dog doesn’t like anymore, or that doesn’t fit to someone else who will love it.


Map of the Event

We’ll be on the sand Rosie's dog beach near the main building where the restrooms and food stands are. We’ll have two large canopies for humans and dogs, along with food, water, and beach blankets.

Sign Up to bring used leashes, collars, or dog accessories to raffle off, or sign up to bring a potluck item to the event!

The potluck is a bring something, eat something event! So please bring some food along if you’re planning to eat so we don’t run out.
